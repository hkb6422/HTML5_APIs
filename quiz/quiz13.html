Canvas Curves Quiz


1.What is the distinct difference between a bezier and quadratic curve?
The bezier curve has two control points whilst the quadratic has one
The quadratic curve has two sides whilst a bezier has one
The quadratic curve accepts five parameters whilst the bezier curve accepts two
2.What parameters does the bezierCurveTo method accept?
bezierCurveTo(cp1x,cp1y,cp2x,cp2y,x,y)
bezierCurveTo(cpx,cpy,x,y)
bezierCurveTo(cpx,cpy)
3.What parameters does the quadraticCurveTo method accept?
quadraticCurveTo(cpx,cpy,x,y)
quadraticCurveTo(x,y,cpx,cpy)
quadraticCurveTo(cp1x,cp1y,cp2x,cp2y,x,y)
